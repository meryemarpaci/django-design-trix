About triX

Pioneering the future of creative expression through AI and 3D technology

Our Mission

At triX, we believe in the boundless potential of human creativity when enhanced by cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to democratize advanced 3D design and visual processing tools, making them accessible to creators of all skill levels.

We're building a platform where imagination can be transformed into reality with ease, breaking down the technical barriers that have traditionally limited creative expression in the digital space.

Through our AI-powered tools, we aim to revolutionize how people create, collaborate on, and share three-dimensional art and designs.

Our Story

The Genesis

triX began in 2022 as a collaborative project between artists and AI researchers who shared a vision of making advanced creative tools more accessible. What started as an experimental platform quickly gained attention for its intuitive approach to complex design challenges.

The Evolution

By early 2023, we had expanded our team and capabilities, introducing breakthrough AI algorithms for inpainting and texture generation. Our community of users grew as word spread about the unique creative possibilities our platform enabled.

Today & Tomorrow

Today, triX stands at the forefront of creative technology, constantly pushing boundaries and redefining what's possible in digital design. Our vision for the future includes even more seamless integration between human creativity and AI assistance, expanding into new realms like augmented reality and collaborative virtual spaces.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

We believe everyone has the capacity for creativity, and our mission is to provide tools that amplify and extend these natural abilities.

Advanced technology should be intuitive and accessible to all, regardless of technical background or experience level.

The best ideas emerge when diverse perspectives come together. We foster collaboration both within our team and in our user community.
